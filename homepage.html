DefenSys AI

DefenSys AI is an intelligent cybersecurity platform that detects, analyzes, and prevents digital attacks using machine learning and deep learning techniques. It integrates real-time data from network traffic, system logs, and emails to classify threats like DDoS attacks, malware activity, phishing attacks, and insider threats with high precision. With a dynamic dashboard and self-improving AI core, DefenSys AI not only responds to attacks but gets ahead of them.
